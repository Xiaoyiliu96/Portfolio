GAME BACKGROUND

This is a maze game that tells the story of a 10-year-old girl who failed to commit suicide and became a persistent vegetative state. In the game, we can see what happened to the girl initially, and when the player wins, they can also see the girl's story. The first level describes how the girl, after failing to commit suicide, became a vegetative state, as we learn from the doctors' conversations. The player controls the girl to leave the maze. During this, we encounter a person dressed in a rabbit costume who tells the player that his key is missing and asks for help to find it. After finding the key outside a pink house, the player enters and plays the maze game.

The second level describes the girl searching for keys and coins in a grassy maze to clear the level. The third level describes the girl waking up in a hospital room and then walking downstairs to a dark maze, where one of the rooms contains a black coffin, symbolizing that the girl has died. At the end of the game, from the doctors' conversation, we learn that the girl couldn't be saved.

GAME CHARACTER

# Bunny

In the first level, the bunny is a symbolic NPC encountered in the first level. It can speak to the player and stands in front of a locked door, acting as a guide and gatekeeper. The bunny asks the player to find its key, encouraging exploration and teaching basic game mechanics such as collecting gold coins and keys. Its friendly appearance contrasts with the darker themes of the game, symbolizing innocence and lost childhood.

Health Bar
Core Mechanic
The player cannot restore health. Traps and hazards reduce health by 20% or 100%. If health reaches 0%, the player dies and returns to the main menu.
Permanent Damage System
Coins
Collectible
Coins are required to unlock doors and advance to the next level. Players must explore the environment carefully to find them.
Bunny’s Key
Quest Item
A hidden key that unlocks the door to the next stage. Without it, progression is impossible, encouraging full exploration.
Trap
Hazard
Each trap reduces health by 20%. Since health cannot be restored, repeated mistakes will quickly lead to death.

How to play the game?

It is a maze game, the player can find the coins and key in this game. Press the space bar to jump. Press "M" on the keyboard to pause the game. or exit the game. Press "P" on the keyboard to check the map. You can see where you are by jumping above. 20% of the damage will be taken when encountering a trap. (If your HP becomes 0 will be forced to jump to the Main Menu page) This is a maze game to find an exit. Victory conditions: Find the key or coins.
