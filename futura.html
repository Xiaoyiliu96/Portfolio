About
  • Title: Future
  • Date: 2022 Fall
  • Design Tool: Adobe InDesign
  • Class: Art29500 Typography I
  • Colors: #C11F1D, #5B595A, #333333
  • Text Content: History and Evolution of Future Typeface
  • Idea: This design uses vibrant red for the first word of each paragraph to draw attention to key points and to highlight the word "Future" in the title, creating a focal point. The black words background enhances the contrast and modern aesthetic, while the sleek abstract shapes and additional graphics reinforce the futuristic theme.
