My Favorite Projects

  • Game Design Project: Maze Game
     
    I used unreal engine to creat maze game. This game background is talking about a 10-year-old girl who failed to commit suicide and became a persistent vegetative state. In the game, we can see what happened to the girl initially, and when the player wins, they can also see the girl's story. The first level describes how the girl, after failing to commit suicide, became a vegetative state, as we learn from the doctors' conversations. The player controls the girl to leave the maze. During this, we encounter a person dressed in a rabbit costume who tells the player that his key is missing and asks for help to find it. After finding the key outside a pink house, the player enters and plays the maze game. The second level describes the girl searching for keys and coins in a grassy maze to clear the level. The third level describes the girl waking up in a hospital room and then walking downstairs to a dark maze, where one of the rooms contains a black coffin, symbolizing that the girl has died. At the end of the game, from the doctors' conversation, we learn that the girl couldn't be saved. Click me to see more...
