"Animal Crossing: Maze" is a 3D animation project created for the HUW 162: 3D Animation class. The project involves creating a captivating and immersive maze environment inspired by the beloved game "Animal Crossing." The animation seeks to bring the charm and aesthetic of "Animal Crossing" into a new light, presenting viewers with a fun and engaging maze exploration.

The story follows the protagonist of the game, who accidentally drops some coins. In an attempt to retrieve them, the protagonist inadvertently enters a maze. Determined to recover the lost coins and find a way out, the protagonist must navigate through the maze. By jumping up and using their surroundings to spot the exit, the protagonist eventually escapes the maze.
